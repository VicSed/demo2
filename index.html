Our Heritage

Four decades of passion for the extraordinary

Founded in 1984, Amfresh has grown from a single-origin fruit exporter into a global fresh produce leader. Our journey is rooted in a deep respect for the land, the people who cultivate it, and the communities we serve.

Today, we operate across the entire value chain — from breeding and cultivation to post-harvest technology, logistics, and retail distribution. This vertical integration allows us to deliver consistency, quality, and innovation at every step.

Sustainability

Committed to Zero Waste

Our Sustainability Strategy

In 2020, we launched our Group Sustainability Strategy to guide our efforts in enhancing outcomes for both people and the planet.

This strategy focuses on the most relevant sustainability areas for AMFRESH, where we can generate a meaningful and measurable impact across our operations and supply chain.

The Insectarium Program

This initiative is based on breeding natural predators to control pests in our citrus plantations, leveraging the natural food chain and minimizing the use of chemical treatments — always as a last resort.

Through a rigorous and controlled process, we are able to manage pests naturally across 65 hectares of farmland, delivering food with reduced chemical impact to consumers.

Driving Change at Scale

At AMFRESH, we are committed to leading the transition towards better fresh food at a sustainable scale.

As a shared-value company, we actively promote innovation and transformation across our entire value chain and the wider industry.

Zero Waste Commitment

We are committed to achieving 0% food waste in our packhouses.

While waste at farm level can be influenced by uncontrollable factors such as weather conditions, we focus on what we can control: excellence in agricultural management.
